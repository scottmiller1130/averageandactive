## The Build

You need a large platter. Not a bowl ‚Äî a platter. The whole point is thin layers with maximum surface area so every chip gets a little bit of everything. Here's how it goes:

- Layer 1: One can of refried beans, spread thin across the platter. This is the foundation.
- Layer 2: Greek yogurt mixed with a packet of taco seasoning. Spread it right over the beans.
- Layer 3: All the toppings ‚Äî diced tomatoes, green peppers, onions, sliced black olives, shredded cheese, and lettuce on top.
- Serve with tortilla chips. That's it. That's the whole thing.

If you've never swapped Greek yogurt for sour cream, this is your sign. Mixed with taco seasoning, you genuinely cannot tell the difference.
